Themes of Hackathon

Tamil & Technology

Tamil language is the oldest language in the world and it is not just a language, it is a way of living. This classical language had a presence over 5000 years ago and it is spoken by more than 90 million people across the world. Tamil is the official language of the Indian state Tamilnadu. Despite this fact, Tamil is not much used as a language of technology. This track will help you to explore some existing problems to bridge this gap.
